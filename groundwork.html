01 — The Problem

The noise isn't the real problem.

There's always been noise. The real problem is that you've stopped trusting your own thinking. Every new take makes you second-guess yourself. Every prediction makes you panic. You're not looking for information — you're looking for someone to tell you what to think.

That outsourcing is making it worse.

Sound familiar?

  • 01 You've read 50 articles about AI and your career and feel more confused than before
  • 02 You oscillate between "everything's fine" and "my career is over" depending on who you talked to last
  • 03 You overthink every decision and keep waiting to feel certain before you act
  • 04 You know the panic isn't helping but you can't seem to stop it
  • 05 You wish someone would just tell you what to do — but you also know that's not really what you need

From panic to grounded action — one step at a time.

Not a productivity system. Not a course about AI. A structured process for moving through uncertainty — processing what's real, letting go of what isn't, and choosing clear next steps.
